VIFO - ABOUT US
Instant short logistics news

VIFO is a news app in the logistics space that brings to its users a multitude of instant news from multiple sources with live updates, be it local or global. The sole purpose of VIFO is to bring about convenience and empower our users globally. All stories contain headlines and a brief summary. Users can set their preferences from a list of categories. So stay ahead with latest happenings and current affairs in the logistics space anytime, anywhere!

Features:

➢ Short instant news
Browse through short-summarized news to stay updated in the shortest time. No need to subscribe and browse through multiple channels to know what’s happening in the logistics space.
➢ Your feed
More than 15 categories of preferences to choose from at your disposal VIFO will curate Your Feed with topics you care about
➢ News Categories
VIFO curates all types of news and headlines from topics like– markets, finance, insurance, law, ports, ship building, tech & innovation and many more. News is sourced from various categories and various sources, making sure that you always get the best. Get limited daily notifications for the most important news and breaking news of the day
➢ Trending News
Get firsthand access to what’s trending around you and the world beyond. Stay updated with all the breaking news locally as well as globally.
➢ Search
Search lets you find any news, recent or old, by simply typing in the keywords in the search box. No need to keep scrolling to look for an interesting news that you may have missed out.
➢ Bookmark
Want to archive a news for later? No worries, now you can bookmark any news. Access it anytime by simply going in the bookmarks section.
➢ Share
Share any news with your friends and colleagues through WhatsApp, Facebook, LinkedIn, google plus and more
➢ Road ahead
This is just the beginning. We have much more in store for you. Stay tuned, stay updated.
